Among the most excellent skilled occupational therapist in the city, our clinic is offering exceptional services in the field of occupational therapy, speech therapy.

Shaping Neuro skills- Occupational therapy clinic specializing in neuro care of children as well as adults helping children of ADHD, SPD(Secondary processing disorder), Autism, Developmental delay, Oro-motor disorder, Speech, Feeding and eating disorder of infants and childhood Learning Disability stroke and Parkinson's.
